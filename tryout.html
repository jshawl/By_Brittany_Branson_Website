By Brittany Branson

Let's make some pretty together, shall we?

That's, at least, what I always try to do! I have always been an artist, but I fell back in love with painting after having had the chance to create some pieces for my own wedding. As an arts manager and web developer, I'm always surrounded by creative and inspiring people. But, nothing beats working a canvas with your own hands and imagination.

My style is light, flowy, even a bit ethereal. My two favorite tools? White paint and my fingers. Yep, I prefer to paint with my fingers over my brushes! The outcome: a totally custom, totally unique, totally pretty piece (or pieces) you and your significant other can keep and display for a lifetime.

When not creating paintings and crests, you can find me in the kitchen cooking up my signature steak au poivre, taking in the glory that is my favorite vanilla candle, working the world wide web, and enjoying weekend trips with my pilot hubby Tim.
